Bouncy

  • High Concept: A challenging and rewarding puzzle game based on physics and collisions. You must become one with the bouncy ball launcher.
  • Player Experience Goals: To have the player feel challenged, but not get too frustrated with the interesting puzzles.
  • Genre: Puzzle/2D Shooter
  • Game play: The player has control of a gun/launcher that shoots a limited number of bouncy balls in any direction. With a finite set of shots, tactful placement and problem solving skills will progress the player from level to level.
  • Prototype goal: Demonstrate the possibilites of puzzles that could be created and the simple yet entertaining game mechanics.
  • Features: Smooth, friendly graphics and an awesome soundtrack. Different types of bouncy balls which have different levels of bounciness combined with the power to choose the starting velocity of each ball will allow the player to come up with creative and satisfying solutions to the problems at hand. Multiple different game modes like Time Attack, Endless Bouncy Balls and Classic will keep the player coming back for more.
  • Setting/Premise: This game is not story driven at all, so the setting is completely contained without any resemblance to reality. The world consists of various different surfaces on which to ricochet off of, and elements such as wind of glue to alter the bounce intensity on different sections.
  • Story: N/A
  • Target Audience: Any casual gamers who have problem solving abilities required by Nancy Drew: Treasure in the Royal Tower or Angry Birds.
  • Play time: ~5 minutes
